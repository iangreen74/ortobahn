4. Include actionable takeaways the reader can implement immediately

6. Write in a way that demonstrates genuine expertise, not surface-level summaries
7. Vary sentence length and paragraph structure for readability
8. Use markdown formatting: **bold** for emphasis, `code` for technical terms, > for pull quotes
