The Content Pipeline

1

Analytics

Analyzes past performance — engagement rates, top-performing themes, audience signals — to ground every decision in data.

2

CEO

Sets strategic direction: brand themes, tone, posting cadence, and priorities — informed by analytics and trending topics.

3

Strategist

Turns strategy into a concrete content plan — specific post ideas, hooks, angles, and platform targeting.

4

Creator

Writes platform-native content — respecting character limits, tone, and format conventions for each network.

5

Publisher

Confidence-gates every post, then publishes directly to connected platforms. Low-confidence drafts are held for review.

The Operations Layer

6

SRE

Monitors system health, pipeline reliability, platform API status, and token usage around the clock.

7

CFO

Tracks costs, ROI per post, and budget efficiency. Enforces spending limits and makes financial recommendations.

8

Ops

Manages client onboarding, profile enrichment, scheduling, and resource allocation across all accounts.

9

Marketing

Generates marketing content for Ortobahn itself — using real platform metrics as proof points. The company markets itself.

Built for Autonomous Operation

Multi-Platform Publishing

Bluesky, Twitter/X, and LinkedIn — each post tailored to platform conventions, character limits, and audience expectations.

Confidence Gating

Every post gets a confidence score. Only content above your threshold publishes automatically — the rest waits for human review.

Brand Voice Control

Define your brand's tone, themes, and boundaries. The AI stays on-brand across every post and every platform.

Analytics Feedback Loop

Performance data feeds back into the next cycle. The system learns what works and adapts strategy automatically.

Multi-Tenant

Manage multiple brands from one instance. Each client gets their own strategy, voice, and publishing schedule.

Trend-Aware

Integrates Google Trends, NewsAPI, and RSS feeds to surface relevant topics — content stays timely and engaging.
